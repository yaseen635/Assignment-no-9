Nested List Example
  1. Fruits
    A fruit is the mature, typically sweet or sour, edible part of a flowering plant that contains seeds. It develops from the flower and aids in seed dispersal.
    • Apple
    • Mango
    • Banana
  2. Vegetable
    A vegetable is an edible part of a plant, such as leaves, stems, roots, or flowers, that is typically savory or less sweet than fruit.
    • Carrot
    • Tomato
    • Potato
  3. Dairy Product
    A dairy product is any food made from milk.
    • Milk
    • Cheese
    • Yougrt
  4. Snacks
    Snacks are small portions of food typically eaten between meals, often quick and easy to consume, and can be either sweet or savory.
    • Chips
    • Biscuits
    • Chocolates
  5. Beverages
    Beverages are drinks, typically liquids, that are consumed to quench thirst or for enjoyment.
    • Tea
    • Coffee
    • Juice
